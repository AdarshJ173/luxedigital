FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions we receive from local shop owners about our premium website services.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of your website. Typically, our starter websites take 2-3 weeks, premium websites take 4-6 weeks, and custom projects can take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you offer ongoing website maintenance?

Yes, we offer several maintenance packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date and performing at its best. Our packages include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, and priority support. We can tailor a maintenance plan to suit your specific needs and budget.

What if I need help after my website launches?

All our websites come with 30 days of free support after launch. Beyond that, we offer different support packages to meet your ongoing needs. You can also purchase individual hours of support as needed. We're always here to help you get the most from your website.

Can I update my website content myself?

Absolutely! We build most websites with a content management system (CMS) that allows you to easily update text, images, and products without any technical knowledge. We'll provide training and documentation to help you manage your content confidently.

Do you provide hosting services?

Yes, we offer premium hosting services optimized for performance and security. Our hosting includes automatic updates, daily backups, security monitoring, and 24/7 technical support. However, if you prefer to use your own hosting provider, we can accommodate that as well.
